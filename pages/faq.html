Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chascent all about?
Chascent is a curated showcase of handmade goods — highlighting natural materials, simple forms, and timeless craft. It’s a place for slow-made inspiration, not a store.
Are the products available for purchase?
This site doesn’t process orders. Some featured pieces may be available for purchase through other shops or makers. If available, links or contact info may be provided.
Where are these handmade items from?
The featured items are either created in-house or selected from small makers with a strong focus on sustainability, craftsmanship, and authenticity.
Do you offer custom orders?
Custom orders are not processed directly on this site. However, you can reach out through the contact section if you have a specific inquiry — we may refer you to the right maker.
Are the materials really eco-friendly?
Yes. Items featured are chosen for their use of eco-conscious elements — such as bamboo, cotton rope, natural ceramics, or reclaimed materials.
<
Is Chascent a physical store?
We collaborate with independent makers. Items are displayed in a shared studio, and you may visit by appointment.
How can I care for the items?
Each product description offers simple care tips. In general, avoid harsh cleaners, and store in dry areas. Gentle use keeps natural items beautiful longer.
